About-Us

Hello, This is SEE...

Society of Electronics Engineers [SEE] is one of the core ocieties of the ECE Department. The prime focus of SEE is to provide a friendly environment for the students to learn and groom their technical as well as soft skills. SEE regularly organises various technical and non-technical events. Technical events like workshops, seminars, guest lectures, quizzes etc. spread awareness about the latest technology, trends and industrial requirements. While the non-technical events like writing competition, singing competition, social quizzes, debates and cultural programs provide a platform for students willing to showcase their diverse skills. These events also serve as a self learning environment for the members and help them to develop & refine certain skills like teamwork and collaboration, event management and organization. The members also improve their communication and presentation skills which aid the overall development of their personality. Team SEE always strives to work hard to accomplish the tasks given to us with perfection. SEE along with Robotics lab and many other collaborators organise the national level robotics event 'Robochamps' every year in which students from different colleges actively participate to show off their robotics skills. SEE is also behind the design and publishing of 'ELECTROCOM'. All this is done under the excellent guidance of our faculty head Mr.Praveen Chakravarti who constantly motivates and supports the members to do better, learn more and grow as a stronger team.
